   Attribute information:

   Input variables (based on physicochemical tests):
   1 - fixed_acidity - grams of tartaric acid per cubic decimeter
   2 - volatile_acidity - grams of acetic acid per cubic decimeter
   3 - citric_acid - grams of citric acid per cubic decimeter
   4 - residual_sugar - grams of sugar per cubic decimeter
   5 - chlorides - grams of sodium chloride per cubic decimeter
   6 - free_sulfur_dioxide - grams of free sulfur dioxide per cubic decimeter
   7 - total_sulfur_dioxide - grams of total sulfur dioxide per cubic decimeter
   8 - density - density of the wine itself (grams per cubic decimeter)
   9 - ph - the pH of the wine
   10 - sulphates - grams of potassium sulfate per cubic decimeter
   11 - alcohol - percentage alcohol by volume
   Output variable (based on sensory data from humans): 
   12 - quality (score between 0 and 10)

   Missing Attribute Values: None
